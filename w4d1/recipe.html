Description

Mexican-style slow-cooked pork shoulder

Ingredients

  • 4 pound pork shoulder
  • 2 cups of chicken brothr
  • 1 tablespoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of coriander
  • 1 tablespoon of ancho chile
  • 4 cloves of minced garlic

Instructions

  1. Mix spices and garlic together in bowl
  2. Coat shoulder in spices
  3. Pour coups of chicken broth into slow cooker
  4. Set dusted shoulder into slow cooker
  5. Turn slow cooker to low and let cook for 10 hours
  6. After five hours, turn the pork.

This recipe originated from Carnitas
